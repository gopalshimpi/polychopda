Mahatma Gandhi Shikshan Mandal’s Smt. Sharchchandrika Suresh Patil Institute of technology (Polytechnic) is a realization of a dream by Late Smt. Sharchchandrika Akka (Ex-Education Ministress, Maharashtra State) and her husband Dadasaheb Dr. Suresh G. Patil (Founder President MGSM, Chopda). It’s a premier institute, known for quality education. The institute has been established in 1983 with an aim to provide scope for technical education for the students belonging to rural areas. Now the institute stands tall among its contemporaries and boosts of a reputation that it has been able to maintain over these years. As the mission of the institute goes it prepares students from diverse backgrounds and trains them to face global challenges with instilling the spirit of professionalism in them. Institutes mission is to To prepare students from diverse background to have sprite of professionalism and train them to face future challenges. To satisfy and steer the students to become the best techno-crats to serve the mankind.
