About Branch
                 Civil Engineering branch started since 2009 ,it is  mother of all branches in Engineering. Earlier, there were only two branches Civil Engineering and Military Engineering. Civil Engineering was a pool of all other streams like Mechanical and Electrical and now it has grown into a full fledged branch catering to the multiple needs of the industry and customer. Many pass out students have taken building construction as their career and have acquired reputation. In the last decade number of students seeking admission to Civil Engineering Diploma was rather too low. This has resulted in acute shortage of Civil Engineers. The future of students joining Civil Engineering stream is very bright. Pass out diploma candidates are eligible to work as registered government contractors for the Govt. of Maharashtra. The department is having well experienced staff and well equipped lab facilities. Site visits, seminars, guest lectures are arranged for students development.
